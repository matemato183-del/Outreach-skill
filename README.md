# Outreach — Skill. Turn Strangers into Clients

Most cold messages get ignored. This skill fixes that.

A skill for [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code), [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai/), [Cursor](https://cursor.com/), and other compatible CLI tools. specialized in B2B cold outreach, built around the psychology of why people reply to strangers. It handles everything from first-touch client prospecting to follow-ups, re-pitches, complaints, sponsorship asks, and creator DMs — on any channel, in any language.

## What makes it different

Most outreach tools help you send more messages. This one helps you send better ones.

- **Situational triggers over surface personalization** — references funding rounds, job postings, product launches, and growth signals instead of generic openers
- **Problem-first framing** — every message leads with the recipient's situation, not the sender's pitch
- **Smart CTA progression** — soft CTAs on first touch, hard CTAs later in the sequence
- **Built-in humanizer** — automatic writing filter strips AI patterns, filler phrases, and robotic sentence structure from every output
- **Omnichannel sequences** — generates full 7-touch cadences across Email, LinkedIn, and DMs in one go
- **Works in any language** — responds in your language, writes in the recipient's

## Installation

1. Download `SKILL.md`
2. Place it in your Claude Code skills folder:
```
~/.claude/skills/outreach/SKILL.md
```
sometimes the .claude folder is hard to find... depending on your operating system you'll need a keyboard shortcut, just search it online...
3. Restart Claude Code

   

**Or simply copy the link to this page and ask your AI to install it for you :)**



**No setup required.** No tools, APIs, or additional installs needed. Works immediately after installation.

## Usage

Just describe who you want to reach and why. The skill asks the right questions before writing anything.

Examples:
- *"I want to cold email the Head of Marketing at [company]"*
- *"Write me an Instagram DM to a fitness coach offering my editing services"*
- *"I need a 7-touch email sequence for SaaS outreach"*
- *"My complaint to [company] got no response — help me escalate"*
